Goal

The stories in Бераллин гІенаш provide a vivid cultural and historical picture of the Chechen life as it was decades ago. The translations are aimed at people interested in Chechen life and history, but also at native speakers from the diaspora, to support them in maintaining their beautiful and rich language.

HELP!

Anyone interested in helping out with the draft translation of the stories (from Chechen into English or Dutch) or the brushing-up of the results (to make for natural translations) can contact us at beerallin dot gheenash at solcon dot nl.
