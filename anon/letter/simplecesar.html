Materials

Here is an overview of materials and sites that might be handy for searching syntax:

  • Cesar: a web application to browse and search through syntactically annotated texts, including Chechen.
  • Manual: Cesar's pdf manual
  • Step-by-step: also available is a step-by-step guide
  • Tagset: the Penn-treebank syntax annotation tagset. Note the section on 'word category' annotation (called POS annotation) and on 'constituent annotation' (called Syntactic annotation)
